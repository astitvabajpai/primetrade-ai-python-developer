Contrarian edge in fear
ETH (+$197) and MELANIA (+$218) deliver their peak avg PnL during Extreme Fear — ideal for counter-trend positioning.

Shorts beat longs in every sentiment. Extreme Greed: shorts avg $114 vs longs $10.5 — a 10× PnL gap.
